player's turn:
1. roll the dice
2. if was 7:
 - players >= 8 cards remove half of them
 - player moves knight
 - if knight is next to buildings: player draws random card from one of the adjacent players
3. deal resources to players
4. until the player is done, do one of these:
 - buy a card
 - play a card (only one per turn)
 - build road/settlement/city
 - trade with bank
 - trade with other players
